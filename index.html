About Me

I am a distinguished AI researcher and data scientist with over 10 years of experience developing cutting-edge machine learning solutions across diverse industries including healthcare, finance, and cybersecurity. My expertise spans deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and MLOps, with a strong theoretical foundation in physics and quantum computing.

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of building scalable AI systems, leading technical teams, and translating complex research into production-ready applications. From developing COVID-19 detection systems with 93% accuracy to creating sophisticated trading algorithms and building data science departments from the ground up, I'm passionate about leveraging AI to solve real-world problems.

My academic background includes a PhD in Theoretical Physics from Harvard University and postdoctoral work at MIT under Dr. Scott Aaronson in quantum computing and computational complexity theory. This unique combination of theoretical knowledge and practical experience allows me to approach AI challenges from both research and engineering perspectives.
